2. Scope

This policy applies only to the App installed from Google Play, Apple App Store, or other official distribution channels. It does not apply to third-party websites you may open from advertisements inside the App.

3. What personal data we process

3.1 Data stored only on your device (never transmitted to our servers)

  • Approximate or precise location (latitude/longitude) when you grant permission, or coordinates you pick manually — used solely to compute prayer times and Qibla bearing. Stored locally (e.g. cached_location_v1).
  • App preferences: calculation method, madhab, theme, language, time format, notification toggles, notification sound, notification offset.
  • Ephemeral counters (e.g. app open count) used only to decide when to show an optional in-app rating prompt.

3.2 Data processed by third-party processors (not on our servers)

  • Google AdMob may process your device’s advertising identifier (Android Advertising ID / iOS Identifier for Advertisers when permitted), coarse device/region metadata, and ad interaction events to deliver and measure ads and to detect invalid traffic. AdMob operates under Google’s policies: https://policies.google.com/privacy
  • Apple Maps / Google Play Services may process coordinates when you use reverse geocoding to label a city. That processing occurs on the device vendor’s infrastructure under their respective privacy policies. We do not operate a backend that receives those queries for this App.

4. Purposes and lawful bases (GDPR / UK GDPR)

  • Prayer times, Qibla, notifications, and UI personalization — lawful basis: consent (permission dialogs and your continued use of optional features) and, where applicable, contract (delivering the App you installed).
  • Non-personalized or limited ads where consent is not granted — lawful basis: legitimate interests (funding a free app with minimal data) balanced against your rights; you can withdraw ad tracking via system settings.
  • Personalized ads in the EEA/UK — lawful basis: consent via Google’s User Messaging Platform (UMP) before personalized ads load.

5. What we do NOT do

We do not sell your personal information (CCPA/CPRA “sale” or “sharing” for cross-context behavioral advertising as performed by us — we have no data inventory to sell). We do not build cross-app profiles. We do not use analytics SDKs. We do not upload your location file to our own servers. We do not scrape contacts, photos, microphone, or clipboard.

6. International transfers

7. Retention

All on-device data persists until you clear app storage or uninstall. We retain zero personal data on servers we control for this App because no such database exists.

8. Security

We rely on industry-standard device encryption (full-disk encryption provided by iOS/Android) and secure OS sandboxes. No passwords are collected.
